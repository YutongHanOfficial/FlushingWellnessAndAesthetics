About Us

Dr. Vijay Naraparaju, MD FACP is a dual board certified physician with advanced training. He is deeply entrenched in the Genesee County community, having served his residency at Hurley Medical Center, and subsequently practicing Internal Medicine in the Flint area for 13 years.

Dr. Naraparaju’s many years of experience have taught him that patients' lives are happier when they feel and look their best! So, to assist women and men in the Flint area in becoming more confident in their appearance – and achieving a better quality of life – he opened Flushing Wellness and Aesthetics. Here we offer the latest advancements in hormone replacement therapy and non-invasive incontinence treatment, as well as Botox and dermal fillers.

Dr. Naraparaju believes that the most important skill a physician needs is to listen, without judgment. Our motto is to provide empathetic, compassionate, competent, non-judgmental, and confidential care to all patients. If you are interested in improving the quality of your life, look no further than Flushing Wellness & Aesthetics!

Meet Our Staff

Vijay Naraparaju, MD, FACP

I finished my Medical school in India in 1998. After which I did training in Pediatrics. Subsequently I moved to the Caribbean in 2000, where I worked as a clinic tutor at St. George’s University from 2000-2002.

I started my residency in Combined Internal Medicine and Pediatrics in 2003, and graduated in 2007 at Hurley Medical Center in Flint, MI – where I started as faculty and became associate Program Director and then Program Director until December 2019.

Five years ago I started my private practice in Flushing, MI which I bought from Dr. Kinras. I enjoy serving the residents of Genesee County – and helping my patients feel and look their best, so they can live their best lives. I also enjoy giving back to my community by teaching medical students and medical residents.

I believe that the most important skills a physician needs is listening skills and being non judgmental. I am here to provide care for the problems patients present, not to judge them! I am also competent in providing care for the LGBTQ community.

My motto is providing empathetic, compassionate, competent, non judgmental, confidential care to all of my patients.

Board Certified in Pediatrics
Fellow of American Academy of Pediatrics

Christina Aplin-Snider, RN, FNP-BC

Christina Aplin-Snider is a nationally board certified Family Nurse Practitioner with over 18 years experience in family practice and pediatrics. In addition, she has significant experience in ER, urgent care and geriatrics. She is also an Assistant Professor of Nursing at the University of Michigan-Flint School of Nursing. She has taught graduate nurse practitioner students for over 15 years. Christina is also the health consultant for the Early Child Development Center at the University of Michigan-Flint campus.

Christina is also a volunteer nurse practitioner at the Genesee County Free Clinic. She routinely volunteers with RAMUSA to provide health care services to underserved individuals in various locations annually. Christina has also authored several articles on various health topics.

Christina maintains updated medical knowledge by routinely attending medical conferences from area hospitals and Michigan Medicine/The University of Michigan School of Medicine. Christina has a passion for wellness, health promotion and preventing pediatric obesity. She lives in Fenton and her hobbies include yoga, walking, reading, volunteering with dogs, gardening and travel. Christina looks forward to helping you and your family be healthy and well!
