Privacy Policy

Flushing Wellness & Aesthetics is dedicated to upholding the highest standards of privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of personal information. This comprehensive privacy statement outlines our meticulous information gathering and dissemination practices for our esteemed website: https://flushingaesthetics.com.

Respect for Your Privacy
We hold your privacy in the utmost regard. Any information collected from you, including your email address, will be used exclusively to provide you with the requested information. We unequivocally affirm that we will neither disclose nor sell your personal information to any unaffiliated third party. Should you choose to grant us your email address and permission to send additional information, rest assured that you maintain the right to opt out of receiving any further email communications at your discretion, simply by unsubscribing from our email list.

Voluntary Disclosure
At Flushing Wellness & Aesthetics, we recognize that providing personally identifying information, such as your name, telephone number, email address, city, or state of residence, is a voluntary choice and not a prerequisite for using our website. However, kindly note that certain features of the website may be limited or rendered inaccessible if you opt not to furnish specific personally identifying information when prompted. Furthermore, the omission of essential personal details may hinder Flushing Wellness & Aesthetics' ability to effectively respond to your specific requests. Please be advised that our website solely collects personally identifying information that you willingly provide in response to a direct request.
