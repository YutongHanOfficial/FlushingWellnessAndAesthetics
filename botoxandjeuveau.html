Botox & Jeuveau

Botox, and similar injectables like Jeuveau, can literally take years off your appearance – leaving you looking like a younger and more attractive version of you! Flushing Wellness & Aesthetics offers Botox injections for both men and women, to reduce or eliminate moderate to severe frown lines, lines on the forehead, furrows between the eyes, and even crow’s feet.

When a person makes almost any facial expression, the facial muscles contract which eventually causes lines and furrows to develop and worsen over time. But FDA-approved Botox works by blocking the nerve signals to these muscles, which keeps them from contracting. And when the facial muscles relax, lines, furrows, creases and wrinkles disappear! Even better, recent medical studies have shown that, in addition to eliminating existing wrinkles, the use of Botox injections can help prevent new wrinkles from forming!

This is the reason that Botox is the most popular cosmetic medical treatment in the world. In fact, more than six million Botox treatments are performed annually! The amazing wrinkle reducing effects of Botox begin to be visible within a day or two of injection. And maximum improvement in lines and wrinkles typically becomes visible within a week.

If you live in Genesee County or the greater Flint area, schedule a Botox or Jeuveau appointment at Flushing Wellness & Aesthetics – and turn back the clock by eliminating the lines between, around or above your eyes.

Flushing Wellness & Aesthetics

Dr. Vijay Naraparaju, MD FACP is a dual board certified physician with advanced training. He is deeply entrenched in the Genesee County community, having served his residency at Hurley Medical Center, and subsequently practicing Internal Medicine in the Flint area for 13 years.

Dr. Naraparaju’s many years of experience have taught him that patients lives are happier when they feel and look their best! So, to assist women and men in the Flint area in becoming more confident in their appearance – and achieving a better quality of life – he opened Flushing Wellness and Aesthetics. Here we offer the latest advancements in hormone replacement therapy and non-invasive incontinence treatment, as well as Botox and dermal fillers.

Dr. Naraparaju believes that the most important skill a physician needs is to listen, without judgment. Our motto is to provide empathetic, compassionate, competent, non-judgmental, and confidential care to all patients.
